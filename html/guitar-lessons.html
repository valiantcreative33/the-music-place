Learn to Play the Guitar

All Levels & Ages Welcome!

Both the acoustic and electric guitar are taught depending your needs. He always uses the sight reading system. At the end of each book or by month we can learn a popular song in order to make it more fun.

We try to be practical and useful so our lessons focus on sight-reading, lead guitar training, rhythm guitar with power chords and cadences as well so the student can join a band.

We teach several genres such as latin, jazz, blues, and more – in both classical guitar, electric and acoustic guitars. Beginner students can expect to work on chords, simple songs, melodies, and theory.
